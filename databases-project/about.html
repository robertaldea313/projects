Inspired by the rose-hued buildings of old Marrakech,
palatial Amanjena is close to the fabled Red City,
set in its own expanse of gardens and grand open spaces.

Shaded courtyards with fountains lead
to the central bassin that nourishes the gardens in which
spacious standalone guest pavilions and maisons rest,
all with private gardens and many with private pools.

Views stretch across the olive groves and palms of the oasis,
extending to the High Atlas Mountains on the horizon.
